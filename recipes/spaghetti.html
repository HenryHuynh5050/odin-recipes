Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garilic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine beef, onion, garlic and green pepper. Cook.
  3. Stir tomato products into the pan.
  4. Simmer for an hour, don't forget to stir.
  5. Serve.
