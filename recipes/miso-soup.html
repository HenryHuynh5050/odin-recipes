Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons dashi granules
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 package silken tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces

Steps

  1. Combine water and dashi granules in saucepan. Heat and boil.
  2. Reduce heat and whisk in miso paste.
  3. Stir in tofu.
  4. Add green onions.
  5. Simmer for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
